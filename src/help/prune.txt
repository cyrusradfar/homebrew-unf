By default, prunes snapshots older than 7 days for the current project.
After deleting snapshots, garbage-collects unreferenced blobs.

Examples:
  unf prune                          Prune current project (7-day default)
  unf prune --older-than 30d         Keep only last 30 days
  unf prune --dry-run                Preview what would be deleted
  unf prune --all-projects           Prune every registered project
  unf prune --all-projects --dry-run Preview for all projects

Time formats: 5m (minutes), 2h (hours), 1d (days)
